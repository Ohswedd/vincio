# Refund Policy

Customers on the Pro plan may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase with no processing fee. Basic plan customers have a 14-day refund window and a $5 processing fee.

## Refund windows

| Plan  | Window  | Fee |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Pro   | 30 days | $0  |
| Basic | 14 days | $5  |
